Pig üê∑ Game

  1. The game has 2 players, playing by rounds.
  2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each result gets added to their round's score.
  3. The player can choose to HOLD, which means that their ROUND score gets added to their GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  4. The first player to reach the target score wins the game.
  5. If a player rolls the dice and one of the dice is a 1 then the player's round score is brought back to ZERO and will receive a STRIKE.
  6. If a player receives 3 STRIKES within their turn then they've BUSTED and will lose their GLOBAL score and be brought back to ZERO ending their round.
  7. If the player rolls two 1's then they've roll SNAKE EYES which will automatically bring their GLOBAL score to ZERO and ending their round.
